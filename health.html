Connect your Garmin

This page talks to a small backend (the garmin-proxy folder) that logs into your Garmin account with your email/password using an unofficial library and serves your daily stats as JSON. By default this points at the hosted copy on Render, which works from any device. Your credentials only ever live in that backend's environment — never in this page's JavaScript.
  1. Hosted (default): already set below, pointing at the Render deployment — works on your phone too
  2. Local instead: in garmin-proxy/, copy .env.example to .env, fill in your Garmin email + password, run pip install -r requirements.txt once then python server.py, and paste its local URL below (e.g. http://localhost:8734)
